Our Nap

Our Nap,
Who art in bed,
hallowed be Thy rest;
Thy sleep will come;
Thy doze be done on ground as it is in bed.
Give us this day our daily REM;
and forgive us our awakes
as we forgive those who awoke against us;
and lead us not into arousal,
but deliver us from caffiene. Anap.
